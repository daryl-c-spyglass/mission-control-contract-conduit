# Fix: Remove Redundant Buttons from My Assets Empty State

## Problem

The "My Assets" empty state has "Create Graphics" and "Create Flyer" buttons that are redundant since these options already exist in the Quick Actions section above.

## Solution

Remove the buttons from the empty state. Keep it simple - just show a message directing users to Quick Actions.
