## Issue 1: Auto-Select Missing on Social Media Format

Currently "Auto-Select" button only appears for Print Flyer. Add it to Social Media as well.

1. Show "Auto-Select" button for BOTH formats:
   - Social Media: Auto-selects 1 best photo (Best Exterior/Front)
   - Print Flyer: Auto-selects 3 best photos (Exterior, Kitchen, Living Room)

2. Selection logic by format:

   **Social Media (1 photo):**
   - Select: Best "Front of Structure" OR "Back of Structure" photo
   - Criteria: Highest confidence + highest quality score
   - Fallback: First image if no exterior found

   **Print Flyer (3 photos):**
   - Photo 1: Best Exterior/Front
   - Photo 2: Best Kitchen
   - Photo 3: Best Living Room
   - Fallback: Next best room types, then first images
