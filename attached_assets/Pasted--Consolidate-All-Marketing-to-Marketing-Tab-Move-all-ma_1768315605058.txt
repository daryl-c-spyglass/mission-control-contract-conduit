**2. Remove "Flyer" button from header**

**3. Remove "Create Marketing Materials" from Overview tab**

| Location | Before | After |

- **Want any marketing materials?** → Go to Marketing tab
  - Social media graphics → "Create Graphics" button

- **Browse template designs?** → Go to Templates tab

- CreateFlyerDialog component or functionality
- MarketingMaterialsDialog component or functionality
- Templates tab
- Marketing tab asset display/gallery
- Any API endpoints

### Benefits:
- Single destination for all marketing creation
- Cleaner header (no marketing buttons)
- Clear separation of concerns (Marketing tab = marketing, Overview = property info)
- Users know exactly where to go for marketing materials
